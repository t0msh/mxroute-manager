# Themes

MXroute Manager ships with **10 color themes**: six dark and four light. Each theme restyles the login screen, sidebar, cards, buttons, and accents across the whole app.

Your choice is saved in the browser (localStorage) per device. Delegated users can pick their own theme without affecting anyone else.

Change theme anytime under **Settings**, at the bottom of the page.

![Theme picker in Settings](images/app-tour/settings-themes.png)

## Branded reset portals

Themes above apply to the **main manager app** and its login page. **Branded password reset portals** (e.g. `reset.example.com`) use a separate theme chosen per domain under **Domains → Password Reset Portal**. That public page does not follow your workspace theme.

See [Password reset - Branded portals](password-reset.md#branded-reset-portals) and the [App tour - Domains](app-tour.md#domains) for examples.
